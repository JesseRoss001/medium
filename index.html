About Me

Hello! I'm Dale Hartnell, and I'm excited to share my journey as the owner of Shining Light Psychic Medium with you!

Growing up, life was a bit of a challenge for me. I left school around the age of 14 or 15, and making friends was tough. I often felt different when it came to friendships and relationships, which was hard at times.

From a young age, I could see and sense things that others couldn't, and it was both fascinating and a little scary. I didn't share my feelings or struggles much, which left me feeling quite alone. Holding down a job was tricky too, and I couldn't figure out why. There were moments when I found myself in hospitals, feeling overwhelmed by everything I was experiencing.

Then, everything changed in 2021! While browsing Facebook one day, I stumbled upon a psychic page that truly caught my attention. Meeting a psychic medium who understood what I was going through was such a relief! He offered me both spiritual and mental support, and I even had the chance to attend his very first workshop. That experience opened so many doors for me, and I haven't looked back since!

Now, I help manage his page and explore various workshops to continue growing and learning.

Thank you for taking the time to read a bit about my story!
